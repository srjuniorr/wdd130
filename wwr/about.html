Welcome to our rafting adventure company. We are dedicated to providing the best whitewater experiences.

History

Founded in 1995 by a group of adventurous paddlers, our company began with a single inflatable vessel and an unwavering commitment to exceptional water expeditions.

Through expert paddling techniques and rigorous training in aquatic safety, we've grown into the region's premier provider of exciting river journeys. Our mission remains unchanged: to deliver unforgettable experiences and champion the spirit of outdoor adventure.
